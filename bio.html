Work arrived immediately having graduated from the Foundation, then two year Diploma with A.C.T Drama School Brighton.

My first job entailed touring nine T.I.E. productions for six months and I thrived off the camaraderie, commitment and stamina needed to resonate with multiple characters in three daily shows. Improvisational skills, energy and versatility were essential in playing the sassy ingénue, historical and Shakespearean heroines, dark and light fairytale characters with equal aplomb.

My love, talent and instinct for the stage has driven and enabled steady theatrical work over ten years with six professional Theatre Companies and have regularly been involved in major cultural festivals.

Versatile, with a wide playing age, I’m not scared to delve deep into the dark, light and comic world with required passion, calm instinct and sense of play. I deeply respect and rejoice in the powerful theatrical breathe an ensemble can bring to our community which has led me to set up my own children’s theatre group which is linked to the local theatre.

To date as an actress I have pro-actively carved out a career without an agent I now seek to open more doors and expand my work on stage, film and adverts.

Directors have taken 'my troup' to award winning, five star productions to Edinburgh, Brighton and Glastonbury Festivals.

I'm an open person with a deep emotional world who is never happier than when I'm researching my character's background and inhabiting their world. thereby allowing a three dimensional, spiritual, physical and vocally layered stage presence, to radiate.

My ability to absorb character and follow authentic impulses I believe, comes from listening skills, understanding the context, relinquishing ego and commitment...a blissful space...and respecting the rehearsal space like a place of worship all make for a fine foundation for a director to work from.
